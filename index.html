About Me

I'd like to parlay my skills in design and creating into an exciting career in UX. I studied Graphic Design and Product Design at Pennsylvania State University. I have since used those skills as a freelance designer for various recording artists and as a small business owner. UX, for me, is a tremendous form of expression that thoughtfully engages an audience. It allows me to tinker and manipulate my work-- it allows me to surprise myself with fresh and interesting concepts while moving towards new pinnacles. UX should be clear, concise, and easily read. But more importantly, it should be fun and charismatic.



I have been volunteering at Neighborhood Bike Works for a decade. I'm an avid, award-winning homebrewer of 12 years with recipes ranging from; SMASH (single malt & single hops) to better understand my ingredients, to experimentations in envelope-pushing territories such as a Dandelion Saison and a back-sweetened blueberry wheat beer. I am a Guinness World Record holder: longest distance to shoot a pumpkin (5545 ft). I've been building computers for 6 years. I'm an athlete and outdoorsman (completed through-hike of the Appalachian Trail from Maine to Georgia in 2008 and ran two marathons with a PR of 3:51:45). Also, I achieved the rank of Eagle Scout.
